About SriRam Industries

Founded in 1985 by a group of highly enterprising youngsters guided by an expert semiconductor technologist in india, SriRam Industries has been designing and manufacturing solder wafers and preforms for the semiconductor industry in India. The semiconductor device technology is based on very high standards of purity and cleanliness of input raw materials as well processing and packaging. Thus Sri Ram Industries also had to eveolve and maintain stringent levels of quality of raw materials and cleanliness in the production of alloys and preforms. Precision and accuracy of dimensions and weight have become the life breath of Sri Ram Industries.
